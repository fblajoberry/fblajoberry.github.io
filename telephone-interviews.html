Telephone Interview

Some employers will call the individual before bringing them into the company for an interview; this is known as a telephone interview. They will determine if you are qualified for the position during the call. The purpose of this call is to pre-screen the candidate. You should answer their questions as if you are having a face to face interview, as this call will determine if you are qualified or not. Here are some suggestions to follow.

Keep a copy of your resume nearby and a copy of the job description

Make sure you have a paper and pen nearby to take notes

You need to be focused and organized, know when is a good and bad time for the employer to call you.

While you are on the phone, sit straight and smile. You will come off as positive and clear.

Try to keep your answers concise and to the point.

Support all the points you put on your resume and talk about past achievements

You should have already researched the company. Make sure you indicate to your employer that you have taken the time to research about their company as this will express your interest.

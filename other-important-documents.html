Resumé

You should already have your resumé completed. If not, click here to learn more.

Cover Letter

You should never send your resumé to a potential employer without a cover letter. Here, you should introduce yourself and your resumé to your employer. Remember to keep your paragraphs short and include the correct information of the employer.

Reference List

A reference list provides the employer with sources that they can use to back-up your credentials. It is good to provide at least three references of people who you feel will provide positive information about yourself.

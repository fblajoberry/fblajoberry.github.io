Interviews

What to do before an interview

  • Get a good night's sleep and eat a healthy breakfast
  • Practice answering questions by doing a "mock interview" with a friend or family member.

    • What to do during an interview

    Click here for the Dos and Don'ts of Interviews!

  • Allow the interviewer to ask questions, and keep your answers precise and to the point
  • Indicate your interest in the company by showing the employer that you have taken the time to research their company
  • Be yourself!

    Telephone Interviews

    Sometimes, employers will call the candidate for a telephone interview, or a pre-screening. This call will help the employer determine if the candidate is qualified for the job.

  • Keep a copy of your resumé and the job description nearby
  • Keep a good posture, and make sure you speak clearly into the microphone

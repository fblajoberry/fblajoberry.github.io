What to do before an Interivew

Tips and Tricks to get you interview ready

Be prepared. Make sure to research and be aware of the company's goals, and services that they provide.

Make sure you have a good idea of what type of questions you would like to ask during the interview.

Make a copy of the job responsibilities and become familiar with them.

Neatly type or have a note of your references. Additionally, make sure you contact and inform them of the upcoming interview.

Practise sample interview questions. The more you practise the better prepared you will be.

Reflect on your past accomplishments, work ethics, skills, and characteristics. Determine how you want to display this information to your interviewer.

Prepare your outfit the night before.

Confirm the date, time, and location of your interview beforehand. Make sure to arrive 15 min early.
